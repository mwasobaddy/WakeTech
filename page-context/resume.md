# Résumé Page

## Purpose
Provide a downloadable, detailed professional history for recruiters or clients.

## Content

- **Header**: Name, title, contact details, links to LinkedIn and GitHub.
- **Summary**: 2–3 sentence professional summary.
- **Technical Skills**: Categorized list (Languages, Frameworks, Tools, Platforms).
- **Work Experience**: Company, role, dates, bullet points of achievements.
- **Education**: Degree, institution, year.
- **Certifications**: List with icons.
- **Projects**: Brief bullet list with links.
- **Download**: Button to download PDF résumé.

## Layout

- Two-column on desktop: left sidebar (skills, education, contact) `w-1/3 border-r pr-6`, right main content `flex-1 pl-6`.
- On mobile: stack all sections.
- Use headings `text-xl font-bold text-[#1A1A1A] mb-4`, body `text-[#6C757D]`.
- Simple, clean, high readability.
